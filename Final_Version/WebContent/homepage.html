Rent-A-Sport



This web application allows users to rent sporting equipment to enhance active consumers so minimize the time, financing,
and hassle of purchasing equipment. The users therefore don't have to fully commit to purchasing these items and can simply
rent them for their convenience. We will be operating solely online and our equipment will be shipped from our warehouse.
This is convenient for users who want to try out a lifestyle or sport without the commitment. Users create an account and
simply rent sport bundles or individual equipment. Consumers may think that if they want to go camping one time during a year,
there should be a service where they can simply rent that equipment to try out camping. This is where our business comes in
and gives consumers that option.
